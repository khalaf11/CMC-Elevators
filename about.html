Our Projects
Our company is an Egyptian-Turkish technical cooperation company with experience in designing and manufacturing elevators , the company was able to develop its goals from an importer of Turkish and European elevators to a partnership to transfer modern manufacturing methods on which the European full package elevator industry . That's by using engineering programs to analyze the results and make simulations and then implement them on the latest CNC machines that give high accuracy to our products after operation. Our company provides the full package in Egypt, Libya, Algeria, Tunisia and soon in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, Kuwait and the Sultanate of Oman, with the company's prefabricated metal towers that make the elevator a single connected unit that works in harmony and stability, in addition to the cabins with European specifications and branded machines for the largest and most famous machinery companies. After many regular and complex processes, CMC has accumulated a wealth of technical experience in manufacturing and installation, and has set up an efficient R&D platform which can ensure the stability of the structure design, smooth operation and safety of the elevator.
